Our School Structure

A. Dedicated Teachers

We have a group of dedicated teachers who volunteer their time and teach with passion and heart.

B. Student Mentors

Senior students who benefited from the school help junior students with their studies and schoolwork.

C. Aim

Taking a step to build People, Society, and Nation.
This initiative helps needy students enhance their education, especially those willing to study but lacking financial support.
Currently, we are helping around 25-30 students.

F. Finance

We raise small funds through friends and relatives to sustain the organization.

G. Services

Helping students to become confident and hard-working individuals.
