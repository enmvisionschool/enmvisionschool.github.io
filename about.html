History

ENM Vision School was established in 2010 by Ashutosh Kumar and his wife with only 4 students, driven by the desire to enhance education for economically weak students with a passion to study. It is a non-profit organization.

Aim

Taking a step to build People, Society, and Nation.
This initiative helps needy students enhance their education, especially those willing to study but lacking strong financial support.
Currently, we are helping around 25-30 students.
